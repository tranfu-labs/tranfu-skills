# 头条号明亮科技流程

## Purpose

Create fast-scanning Toutiao visuals for technology concepts, product mechanisms, short processes, and practical checks. The style should feel credible and product-grade without resembling a specific campaign or product.

## Visual Character

Use a high-key near-white field, low visual noise, decisive hierarchy, and one clear directional story. Balance a typographic anchor with an original soft-3D metaphor, but let meaning determine their positions. Preserve generous whitespace and a polished studio finish.

## Palette Roles

- `canvas.luminous`: neutral near-white field with only a faint same-family light shift.
- `ink.graphite`: primary headline and structural text.
- `ink.silver`: secondary explanations and subdued labels.
- `accent.signal`: one concentrated warm coral-red for emphasis, state, and direction.
- `accent.mist`: a pale tint of the signal color for translucent depth.
- `surface.white`: compact nodes and highlights.
- `surface.silver`: neutral 3D icon material and fine separators.

Do not reproduce a complete source palette or treat exact source values as tokens.

## Typography

Use a modern geometric sans-serif character. Headlines are heavy and compact, with a strong jump from display scale to support copy. Supporting text is regular or medium weight with short lines. Labels are concise, visually even, and never paragraph-like.

## Composition Grammar

Use an adaptive asymmetric grid and an 8-unit spacing rhythm. Establish one typographic anchor, one spatial narrative, and one abstract focal object. A clear scan path should connect them, but the axis, anchor positions, node count, and direction change with the content structure. Avoid repeating a fixed split-screen layout.

## Components And Illustration

Use isolated compact rounded nodes, fine single-line connectors, small direct labels, and original silver-gray soft-3D symbols. Use one custom abstract focal sculpture per topic. Do not use a brain, copied source icons, product chrome, large enclosing cards, or literal screenshots.

## Texture And Material

Use bright diffuse studio light, very subtle paper or matte grain, pale contact shadows, translucent highlights, and shallow three-layer depth. Gradients are same-family lighting effects rather than decorative multicolor fills.

## Platform Geometry

Use 1600 x 900 only as the Toutiao design coordinate system. Preserve any accepted native 16:9 raster within ratio tolerance `0.002`, including 1672 x 941, and require a shortest edge of at least 900px. Keep meaningful content inside the proportionally mapped `x=80, y=70, w=1440, h=760` area. A valid top-right brand slot remains in the spec, but branding defaults off, so its reserved area is inactive unless the user explicitly enables branding.

## Debranding And Prohibitions

Generalize exact colors, coordinates, icon silhouettes, and typography identity. Never use or reconstruct the source logo, brand name, headline, topic, brain object, four-node sequence, exact composition, or proprietary assets. Never pass the source image or the selected style reference to an image model. No logo, watermark, model signature, fake brand, page number, placeholder, or visible reserved-area marker.

## Calibration Guidance

The concept image should use a relationship-centered composition, the process image a three-step directional composition, and the checklist image a five-item parallel composition. They must share palette roles, typography character, soft-3D materials, whitespace density, and component language while using visibly different structures.
