# Wave 2: Competitive Analysis

开始前先读 `research-principles.md`。Wave 2 继承 Wave 1 的市场背景。

Wave 2 的目标不是只列出竞品名称，而是回答：

- 谁已经在解决这个问题
- 用户今天真正会拿什么和我们比较
- 现有玩家的弱点和空白在哪里
- 这个市场是否拥挤到难以切入

本 wave 只负责产出 raw research，不直接写综合结论、推荐范围或最终建议。

---

## Research Block B1: Direct Competitors

研究任务：深挖直接竞品，目标通常为 5-8 个。

### 推荐搜索路径

ROUND 1：识别候选玩家

- `{problem} software/app/tool {current year}`
- `best {product category} tools {current year}`
- `G2 {product category} grid`
- `{product category} Product Hunt`

ROUND 2：逐个深挖

- 访问官网看功能、定价、定位
- `{competitor name} review`
- `{competitor name} funding`
- `{competitor name} complaints`

ROUND 3：竞争动态

- `{product category} market share`
- `{competitor 1} vs {competitor 2}`

### 每个竞品至少回答

- Website / Founded / Team size / Funding
- Product / Key features / Integrations
- Pricing model / tiers
- Target customer / positioning
- Traction signals
- Strengths / Weaknesses
- Threat level

## Research Block B2: Indirect Competitors & Substitutes

研究任务：研究用户今天在不用专用工具时，会如何解决这个问题。

### 推荐搜索路径

ROUND 1：现状做法

- `how do {customer type} currently handle {problem}`
- `{problem} without software`
- `{problem} excel template`
- `{problem} outsource`

ROUND 2：邻近产品

- `{adjacent category} that also does {related function}`
- `{major platform} {related feature}`
- `open source {product category}`

ROUND 3：平台风险

- `{major platform} adding {our feature}`
- `{major platform} AI features {current year}`

### 输出内容

- Status quo solutions
- Adjacent products
- Platform risk
- Open source / free alternatives
- Switching cost analysis
- Data gaps

保存到：

- `01-discovery/raw/indirect-competitors.md`

## Research Block B3: Competitor GTM

研究任务：看清竞品是如何获客、销售和留存的。

### 推荐搜索路径

ROUND 1：营销渠道

- 查看竞品官网 blog、resources、case study
- `{competitor name} marketing strategy`
- 搜索广告 / 社媒活动

ROUND 2：销售模式

- `{competitor name} sales team`
- `{product category} sales cycle length`

ROUND 3：内容与 SEO

- `{product category} blog`
- 查看谁在关键词上长期出现

### 输出内容

- Primary acquisition channel
- Sales motion
- Content strategy
- Social presence
- Paid acquisition signals
- Partnerships
- Channel opportunity map
- Key takeaways
- Data gaps

保存到：

- `01-discovery/raw/competitor-gtm.md`
